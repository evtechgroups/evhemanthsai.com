            <p><strong>EVHEMANTH SAI</strong> (born November 29, 2007), also known as <strong>Ellanuru Venkata Hemanth
                    Sai</strong>, is an Indian AI entrepreneur and the founder and CEO of <a
                    href="https://evtechgroups.com">EVTECH GROUPS</a>, a company focused on educational technology
                solutions.</p>

            <h2>Early life and education</h2>
            <p>EVHEMANTH SAI was born in Proddatur, Andhra Pradesh, India to Maheshwarla Nagaraju and Jinka Prasanna
                Lakshmi. He completed his secondary education at <a href="https://davrtpp.org">DAV HIGH SCHOOL,
                    RTPP</a>, where he developed an interest in artificial intelligence and computer programming.</p>

            <h2>Career</h2>
            <p>In 2023, at age 16, EVHEMANTH founded EVTECH GROUPS, an educational technology company developing
                AI-powered learning tools. As CEO, he leads the company's efforts to create personalized learning
                experiences through machine learning and natural language processing technologies.</p>

            <p>His work focuses on making education more accessible through technological innovation. EVHEMANTH has been
                recognized for his entrepreneurial achievements, including being featured on Google as an entrepreneur
                and receiving an invitation to the Global Student Entrepreneur Awards (GSEA).</p>

            <h3>EVTECH GROUPS</h3>
            <p>EVTECH GROUPS specializes in developing artificial intelligence solutions for education. The company's
                products aim to automate administrative tasks for educators while providing personalized learning
                pathways for students.</p>

            <h2>Public recognition</h2>
            <p>EVHEMANTH has been recognized as a young innovator in the field of educational technology. He was
                recognized on
                many Platforms for his expertise in various technologies and also the company was recognized as the top
                7 IT companies in India in april 2025 by <a href="https://www.f6s.com/companies/it/india/co">F6S</a>.
            </p>

            <h2>Personal life</h2>
            <p>EVHEMANTH continues to balance his entrepreneurial pursuits with his education. His work has been noted
                as an example of youth-led innovation in the Indian technology sector.</p>
