EVHEMANTH SAI (born November 29, 2007), also known as Ellanuru Venkata Hemanth Sai, is an Indian AI entrepreneur and the founder and CEO of EVTECH GROUPS, a company focused on educational technology solutions.

Early life and education

EVHEMANTH SAI was born in Proddatur, Andhra Pradesh, India to Maheshwarla Nagaraju and Jinka Prasanna Lakshmi. He completed his secondary education at DAV HIGH SCHOOL, RTPP, where he developed an interest in artificial intelligence and computer programming.

Career

In 2023, at age 16, EVHEMANTH founded EVTECH GROUPS, an educational technology company developing AI-powered learning tools. As CEO, he leads the company's efforts to create personalized learning experiences through machine learning and natural language processing technologies.

His work focuses on making education more accessible through technological innovation. EVHEMANTH has been recognized for his entrepreneurial achievements, including being featured on Google as an entrepreneur and receiving an invitation to the Global Student Entrepreneur Awards (GSEA).

EVTECH GROUPS

EVTECH GROUPS specializes in developing artificial intelligence solutions for education. The company's products aim to automate administrative tasks for educators while providing personalized learning pathways for students.

Public recognition

EVHEMANTH has been recognized as a young innovator in the field of educational technology. He was recognized on many Platforms for his expertise in various technologies and also the company was recognized as the top 7 IT companies in India in april 2025 by F6S.

Personal life

EVHEMANTH continues to balance his entrepreneurial pursuits with his education. His work has been noted as an example of youth-led innovation in the Indian technology sector.
